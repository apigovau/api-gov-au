api.gov.au

About

We are working with all levels of government to collect and document government APIs and other machine-to-machine services in one place.

Here you will be able to discover what APIs are available, how to use them, and understand what data they need.

You will also find guidance on creating and using APIs, and a community discussion forum where you can ask for help

Want to get in touch?

If you work for a government agency and would like to talk about APIs or api.gov.au, send us an email to sbr_tdt@sbr.gov.au

If you don't work in government and have questions about specific APIs, please contact the agency who owns the API for more information on it's availablity, or having it listed on api.gov.au
